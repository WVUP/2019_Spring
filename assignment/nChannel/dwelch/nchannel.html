Which systems do you need to integrate?

BigCommerce

INTUITIVE CUSOMTER EXPERIENCES

Use our BigCommerce connector to provide intuitive customer experiences by offering multiple delivery options and displaying on-hand inventory levels.

Microsoft Dynamics NAV

REAL-TIME INVENTORY MANAGEMENT

Use our Microsoft Dynamics NAV connector to sync inventory between your ERP and all your sales channels. Ensure inventory levels are always accurate and stop overselling.

Magento

CENTRALIZE OPERATIONS

Use our Magento connector for either Magento 1.x or 2.0 to centralize your operations for better order and inventory management. Improve your customer experience so you can overcome your competition.

NetSuite

MULTICHANNEL ORDER MANAGEMENT

Use our NetSuite connector to integrate your ERP with your sales channels. Automate order processing so you can fulfill orders quicker and always deliver on time.

Shopify

SELL MORE ON SHOPIFY

Use our Shopify connector to integrate your webstore with your fulfillment systems. Eliminate the need for manual data entry so you can focus on growing your business.

Lightspeed

TRUE MULTICHANNEL RETAIL

Use our Lightspeed connector to sync your POS and eCommerce channels enabling you to offer buy online, pick up in store. Bridge the gap between offline and online selling.
